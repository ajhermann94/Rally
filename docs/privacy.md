Rally is a motivational alarm app. This policy explains what we collect, why,
and your choices.

## Information we collect
- **Account info:** your email address and a username, when you create an account
  (via email or Sign in with Apple). If you use Sign in with Apple's "Hide My
  Email," we receive only Apple's private relay address.
- **Content you submit:** links (URLs) to TikTok/YouTube clips you submit, and
  related metadata (creator handle, caption, hashtags).
- **App activity:** which clips you save to your Vault, clips you've woken to,
  flags/blocks you create, and your subscription status.
- **Alarm settings:** your alarm time and preferences are stored **on your device**.

We do **not** collect precise location, contacts, health data, or advertising
identifiers. We do **not** use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, and we
do **not** track you across other apps or websites.

## How we use it
- To provide the service: authentication, the daily drop, the Library and Vault,
  clip submission/moderation, and customization.
- To operate subscriptions (Vanguard) and sync your tier across devices.
- To keep the community safe (moderation, flagging, blocking).

We do not sell your personal information.

## Third-party services
- **Supabase** — our backend (authentication, database, functions). Data is
  stored on Supabase infrastructure.
- **Apple** — Sign in with Apple and subscription billing (StoreKit). Apple's
  privacy policy governs those interactions.
- **TikTok / YouTube** — clips are embedded (linked), not hosted by us. When a
  clip loads, the platform may process data per its own privacy policy. We do not
  share your account info with them.

## Data retention & deletion
- We keep your account data until you delete your account.
- **You can delete your account in the app:** Settings → Delete Account. This
  permanently removes your profile, vault, flags, and blocks. Clips you submitted
  to the public Library are anonymized (disassociated from you) and may remain.
- You may also request deletion via our GitHub Issues page ([GITHUB ISSUES URL]).

## Security
Data is transmitted over HTTPS and protected by row-level security so users can
only access their own data. No method is 100% secure.

## Children
Rally is not directed to children and is intended for users **17 and older**
(it includes user-submitted and third-party video content).

## Changes
We may update this policy; we'll revise the effective date above and, for material
changes, notify you in-app.
