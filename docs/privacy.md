        <h1>Privacy Policy — Rally</h1>

        <p><strong>Effective date:</strong> June 27, 2026<br>
        <strong>App:</strong> Rally: Motivation Alarm ("Rally", "we", "us")<br>
        <strong>Contact:</strong> <a href="https://github.com/ajhermann94/rally/issues">open an issue at https://github.com/ajhermann94/rally/issues</a></p>

        <p>Rally is a motivational alarm app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.</p>

        <h2>Information we collect</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Account info:</strong> your email address and a username, when you create an account (via email or Sign in with Apple). If you use Sign in with Apple's "Hide My Email," we receive only Apple's private relay address.</li>
            <li><strong>Content you submit:</strong> links (URLs) to TikTok/YouTube clips you submit, and related metadata (creator handle, caption, hashtags).</li>
            <li><strong>App activity:</strong> which clips you save to your Vault, clips you've woken to, flags/blocks you create, and your subscription status.</li>
            <li><strong>Alarm settings:</strong> your alarm time and preferences are stored <strong>on your device</strong>.</li>
        </ul>
        <p>We do <strong>not</strong> collect precise location, contacts, health data, or advertising identifiers. We do <strong>not</strong> use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, and we do <strong>not</strong> track you across other apps or websites.</p>

        <h2>How we use it</h2>
        <ul>
            <li>To provide the service: authentication, the daily drop, the Library and Vault, clip submission/moderation, and customization.</li>
            <li>To operate subscriptions (Vanguard) and sync your tier across devices.</li>
            <li>To keep the community safe (moderation, flagging, blocking).</li>
        </ul>
        <p>We do not sell your personal information.</p>

        <h2>Third-party services</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Supabase</strong> — our backend (authentication, database, functions). Data is stored on Supabase infrastructure.</li>
            <li><strong>Apple</strong> — Sign in with Apple and subscription billing (StoreKit). Apple's privacy policy governs those interactions.</li>
            <li><strong>TikTok / YouTube</strong> — clips are embedded (linked), not hosted by us. When a clip loads, the platform may process data per its own privacy policy. We do not share your account info with them.</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>Data retention & deletion</h2>
        <ul>
            <li>We keep your account data until you delete your account.</li>
            <li><strong>You can delete your account in the app:</strong> Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, vault, flags, and blocks. Clips you submitted to the public Library are anonymized (disassociated from you) and may remain.</li>
            <li>You may also request deletion via our GitHub Issues page (<a href="https://github.com/ajhermann94/rally/issues">https://github.com/ajhermann94/rally/issues</a>).</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>Security</h2>
        <p>Data is transmitted over HTTPS and protected by row-level security so users can only access their own data. No method is 100% secure.</p>

        <h2>Children</h2>
        <p>Rally is not directed to children and is intended for users <strong>17 and older</strong> (it includes user-submitted and third-party video content).</p>

        <h2>Changes</h2>
        <p>We may update this policy; we'll revise the effective date above and, for material changes, notify you in-app.</p>

        <h2>Contact</h2>
        <p>Questions: <a href="https://github.com/ajhermann94/rally/issues">open an issue at https://github.com/ajhermann94/rally/issues</a></p>
