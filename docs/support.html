FAQ

How does the alarm work?

Set a time on the Lock In tab and tap ARM SYSTEM. Before your set time the screen brightens like a sunrise and music builds; at the set time a motivational video plays. Swipe up and tap "I'M UP" to dismiss. For the full cinematic wake, keep Rally open and charging on your nightstand — a backup notification also fires at the set time.

The video didn't play / had no sound.

Make sure your ringer/silent switch is off, volume is up, and you have a network connection. Some third-party clips restrict embedding or audio; try another.

What is Vanguard?

A subscription ($4.99/month or $34.99/year) that unlocks the Library, full Vault control, clip submission, and customization.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Rally → Cancel. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I restore a purchase?

Settings → Vanguard → Restore Purchases.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, vault, and saved data.

How do I report or block content?

Press and hold any clip in the Library → Report clip or Block creator.
